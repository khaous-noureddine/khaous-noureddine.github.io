Hi! I am a PhD candidate at <a href="https://u-paris.fr/">Université Paris Cité</a> (Paris, France), advised by Prof. <a href="https://gw17.github.io/">Guillaume Wisniewski</a>.

My research focuses on the analysis and interpretation of representations learned by speech foundation models. I aim to better understand what these models learn from large-scale speech data and how linguistic information emerges within their representations.

More broadly, I am interested in developing data-efficient approaches for low-resource languages. I work toward methods that can support field linguists in transcription and documentation, helping improve the inclusivity of speech technologies.
